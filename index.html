Go-2-iterm

Quickly jump from your current Finder location to an iTerm2 tab or window

Download View on GitHub

Hello and welcome !

If you are using iTerm2 on OSX and often switching from the Finder to your favorite shell, Go-2-iterm is probably the app you need.

Workflow

By putting Go-2-iTerm as a shortcut on your Finder, you can now open a new iTerm2 tab or window at the current directory in an easy and neat way.

If you do not put Go-2-iTerm as a shortcut, it will simply launch a shell at the directory of your current focused Finder.
